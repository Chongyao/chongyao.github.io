This paper addresses large-scale eigenvalue problems (e.g., Falcon 9 full structure with 10M nodes, 30M DOFs) by proposing a component modes synthesis method based on multiple partitions. The core innovation is the primal-dual complementary partitioning strategy, which decouples interface nodes of different partitions at coarse scale, significantly improving the accuracy of interface modes. Compared to the traditional Craig-Bampton method, our approach achieves 2-3 orders of magnitude accuracy improvement with the same computational cost.
