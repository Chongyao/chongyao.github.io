This paper proposes a local basis function optimization method to address the ill-conditioned linear system problem caused by badly-shaped mesh elements in finite element simulations. The method reduces the condition number by an order of magnitude while preserving low-frequency modes, improving the efficiency of conjugate gradient solver by 20% on average, up to 87% in benchmarks.
