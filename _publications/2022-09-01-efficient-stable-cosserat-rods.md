This paper presents a novel chain coordinate representation that separates stretching from twisting and bending, using axis-angle representation for the frame field. A tri-block-diagonal preconditioner is designed to accelerate the linear solver convergence. Compared to traditional methods, our approach significantly reduces numerical dissipation and allows larger time steps, achieving better performance.
